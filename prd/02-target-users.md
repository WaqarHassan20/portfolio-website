# Target Users

## Primary Audience

These are the people who will visit and interact with the portfolio:

### 1. Recruiters & Hiring Managers
- Looking for a quick overview of skills and experience
- Want to see real projects with live demos or code links
- Need easy access to resume/CV and contact info

### 2. Potential Clients (Freelance)
- Want to assess design taste and technical quality
- Looking for proof of past work and results
- Need a simple way to get in touch

### 3. Fellow Developers & Collaborators
- Interested in open-source work and tech stack choices
- May want to connect, collaborate, or share feedback
- Will look at code quality and project depth

## Secondary Audience

- **Conference / Community organizers** scouting for speakers
- **Mentors or peers** who were referred to the portfolio

## User Goals

| User | Goal |
|------|------|
| Recruiter | Quickly evaluate fit within 60 seconds |
| Client | Understand capabilities and initiate contact |
| Developer | Explore projects and connect |
