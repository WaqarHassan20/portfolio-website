# Problem Statement

## The Problem

As a developer/designer, I lack a centralized, professional online presence to:

- Showcase my skills as list of tech stack, projects showcase, and experience, about me and contact section to potential employers or clients
- Demonstrate my technical capabilities through a live, well-crafted product
- Stand out in a competitive job market where a GitHub profile alone isn't enough

## Current Pain Points

- No single place that tells my story as a developer
- Recruiters and clients have no quick way to evaluate my work and personality
- Missing a platform to highlight achievements, case studies, and contact information

## The Solution

Build a modern, fast, and visually appealing **personal portfolio website** that serves as a professional hub — one that I fully own, can customize, and reflects my personal brand.
